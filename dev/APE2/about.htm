About Au Petit Escargot

Au Petit Escargot is a French-American home based bakery. We use as many locally sourced ingredients as we can, including crafting a number of ingredients ourselves.

We utilize French techniques with modern inspiration; taking the classics and refining them for today's palates. We primarily focus on French techniques to expose those methods and tastes to everyone. Not everyone can visit France, but through Au Petit Escargot, everyone can have a taste of France.

Our Chef de Cuisine, Anouk, is a French native and studied in numerous French bakeries before moving to America.

Our Chef de Patisserie, Clinton, is a self-taught chef who learned the soul of food through his family. He has honed his skills by working in various restaurants for the last 7 years, along with obtaining advanced degrees in molecular biology. The scope of these degrees allows Au Petit Escargot to incorporate modernist aspects into our cuisine, fusing the old world and the new world together.
